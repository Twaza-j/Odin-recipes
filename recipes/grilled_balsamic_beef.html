Grilled Balsamic Beef


Grilled-Balsamic-Steak

This grilled balsamic beef would be one of my signature dishes if I were a chef at a barbecue restaurant. The aged balsamic vinegar-based marinade is perfectly balanced, and grilling over high heat creates a beautiful sear.

The Ingredients!

  • 1/2 cup aged balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 6 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onion
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1/4 cup fresh rosemary leaves
  • 2 pounds beef top sirloin
  • 4 skewers

Directions

  1. Step 1 Whisk balsamic vinegar, olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, green onion, salt, black pepper, cayenne, and honey together in a bowl. Add rosemary and set aside.
  2. Step 2 Cut beef into cubes about 1 1/4- to 1 1/2- inch square and add to marinade; stir to coat all cubes well. Wrap and refrigerate overnight, tossing occasionally if possible.
